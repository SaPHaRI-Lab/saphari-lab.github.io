We research and create innovative technologies that enhance human wellbeing, through empathetic, responsive, human-centered design across robot and computer interactions.

We advance technologies that foster meaningful social and physical interactions by integrating research with emotional intelligence. Through our mission, with technology we're able to address mental health challenges, provide emotional support, and/or improve our relationship with it.

We are a part of the Human Fusions Institute and Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, OH.
