Opportunities to Join the SaPHaRI Lab

Postdoctoral Researchers

Please email the following to alexis.block@case.edu:

  • Paragraph on why you're interested in the SaPHaRI Lab
  • PDF of your current resume or CV
  • PDF of unofficial transcript (current/most recent program)
  • PDFs of up to three publications
  • Two or more professional references (name, title, institution, email)

Complete applications are reviewed carefully. Expect feedback within one month.

Doctoral Students

  • Apply through CWRU's Electrical, Computer, and Systems Engineering program, or potentially Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering.
  • Indicate interest in working with Dr. Alexis Block.
  • To contact Dr. Block directly before applying, email her and include:
    • Paragraph explaining your interest in the lab
    • Current resume or CV (PDF)
    • Unofficial transcript (PDF)
    • Any relevant publications (PDFs)
    • Two or more references (name, title, institution, email)

Master's Students

  • MS students are typically reviewed after arriving on campus.
  • Lab positions do not usually convert into PhD tracks. Interested PhD applicants should apply directly for doctoral admission.
  • Dr. Block is open to working with MS students on a thesis or voluntary basis. Funding must usually be secured independently.

Undergraduate Students

Undergraduates are welcome to apply! Positions are usually unfunded, but Dr. Block can help you seek funding opportunities.
